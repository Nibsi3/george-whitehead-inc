01 — Practice Area

Commercial &
Banking Law

We advise on the full spectrum of commercial transactions — from complex contract drafting and negotiation to dispute resolution and litigation.

George Whitehead Inc. provides comprehensive commercial and banking law services to a broad range of clients, including national and international businesses, financial institutions, and commercial enterprises.

Our commercial law practice covers the drafting, review, and negotiation of commercial agreements of all kinds, including shareholders' agreements, joint venture agreements, service agreements, supply contracts, and distribution agreements. We also advise on business rescue proceedings and corporate restructuring.

Our banking law expertise encompasses loan agreements, security structures (including mortgage bonds, notarial bonds, and cession of book debts), regulatory compliance, and the enforcement of security in default situations.

Dispute Resolution

We represent clients in commercial disputes before the High Courts of South Africa, as well as in arbitration and mediation proceedings. Director George Whitehead, as a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (FCIArb), brings particular depth to complex commercial arbitration matters.

Services Include

  • Commercial contract drafting & review
  • Shareholders' & joint venture agreements
  • Loan agreements & security structures
  • Business rescue & restructuring
  • Commercial dispute resolution
  • Arbitration & mediation
  • Risk assessments for commercial purchases

02 — Practice Area

Construction Law

Led by Director George Whitehead — MSc Construction Law & Dispute Resolution (King's College London) and Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators — our construction practice is unrivalled in the Southern Cape.

George heads up the construction and commercial division of the practice. He has vast experience in construction and commercial litigation and focuses on disputes and construction claims, mainly in the large-scale civil construction sector.

International Contracts

George specialises in the following international standard form construction contracts:

FIDIC NEC3 NEC4 JCT E5

National Contracts

On a national level, George advises clients on the use of the following standard form contracts:

JBCC COLTO General Conditions Building

Clientele

His clients include national and international construction firms and he also advises clients in the mining sector. George is regularly called upon to advise in risk assessments in commercial purchases and the drafting of bespoke commercial and construction contracts relating thereto.

Services Include

  • Construction disputes & claims
  • FIDIC, NEC3, NEC4, JCT, E5 contracts
  • JBCC, COLTO, General Conditions
  • Construction litigation
  • Arbitration & adjudication
  • Risk assessments
  • Mining sector advisory
  • Bespoke contract drafting

Director Credentials

  • MSc Construction Law & Dispute Resolution — King's College London
  • LLM International Corporate & Commercial Law — King's College London
  • Fellow, Chartered Institute of Arbitrators (FCIArb)
  • Member, Society of Construction Law (UK)

03 — Practice Area

Property &
Conveyancing

Our conveyancing department, led by Associate Loren Giles, provides comprehensive property law services across the Southern Cape — from individual transfers to large-scale developments.

Loren heads up the conveyancing department and is based mainly at the George office. She joined the practice in February 2001 and has gained vast experience in her chosen field during the past 22 years.

Her conveyancing experience includes property transfers (both full title and sectional title), registration of sectional title schemes, bond registrations, as well as miscellaneous matters relating to property — including the registration of servitudes, rights of way, notarial tie agreements, usufructs, sub-divisions and various endorsement applications.

Loren has attended to various large developments in the Southern Cape, including full title and sectional title schemes.

Property Transfers

We handle all aspects of property transfers, from the initial instruction through to registration in the Deeds Office. Our team ensures that every transfer is handled efficiently, accurately, and with the utmost care for our clients' interests.

Bond Registrations

We act for both financial institutions and individual borrowers in the registration of mortgage bonds and notarial bonds, ensuring that all security is properly registered and enforceable.

Services Include

  • Property transfers — full title & sectional title
  • Registration of sectional title schemes
  • Mortgage bond registrations
  • Notarial bond registrations
  • Registration of servitudes
  • Rights of way & usufructs
  • Notarial tie agreements
  • Sub-divisions & endorsement applications
