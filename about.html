Our Story

Who We Are

George Whitehead Inc. is a niche firm of Attorneys and Conveyancers with offices in George and Cape Town, South Africa. The firm was established in 1998 and has since built long-standing relationships with many recurring clients — a testament to the quality of service and personal attention we provide.

We focus on the commercial, banking, property and construction sectors of law, providing tailored legal solutions to a discerning clientele that includes national and international construction firms, financial institutions, property developers, and commercial enterprises.

Our philosophy is simple: every client relationship is a long-term partnership. We do not merely advise — we advocate, protect, and deliver outcomes that endure.
