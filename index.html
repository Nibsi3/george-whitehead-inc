About the Firm

A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Founded in 1998, George Whitehead Inc. has grown into one of the Southern Cape's most respected niche legal practices. We serve a discerning clientele — national and international construction firms, financial institutions, property developers, and commercial enterprises — who demand nothing less than exceptional counsel.

Our philosophy is simple: every client relationship is a long-term partnership. Many of our clients have been with us since inception, a testament to the trust, quality, and personal attention we bring to every matter.

"We do not merely advise — we advocate, protect, and deliver outcomes that endure."

Construction Law
