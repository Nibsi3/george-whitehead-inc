Biography

George heads up the construction and commercial division of the practice, which is based in George with an office in Cape Town.

He has vast experience in construction and commercial litigation and focuses on disputes and construction claims, mainly in the large-scale civil construction sector.

He specialises in international FIDIC, NEC3, NEC4, JCT and E5 construction contracts and on a national level advises clients on the use of the JBCC, COLTO and the General Conditions Building contracts.

His clients include national and international construction firms and he also advises clients in the mining sector. George is regularly called upon to advise in risk assessments in commercial purchases and the drafting of bespoke commercial and construction contracts.

Loren Giles

Associate — Property & Conveyancing

LinkedIn Profile

Biography

Loren heads up the conveyancing department and is based mainly at the George office. She joined the practice in February 2001 and has gained vast experience in her chosen field during the past 22 years.

Prior to this she was a senior assessor at SARS where she concentrated on income tax. She has a strong accounting and finance background.

Her conveyancing experience includes property transfers (both full title and sectional title), registration of sectional title schemes, bond registrations, as well as miscellaneous matters relating to property — including the registration of servitudes, rights of way, notarial tie agreements, usufructs, sub-divisions and various endorsement applications.

Loren has attended to various large developments in the Southern Cape, including full title and sectional title schemes.
